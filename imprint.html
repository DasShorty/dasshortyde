Data Privacy

CloudFlare

We use Cloudflare services on our websites, including dasshorty.de, theccloud.de, shry.site, and colllectyour.team. Cloudflare provides us with a content delivery network (CDN) and various security features to enhance the performance and security of our websites. What Information Does Cloudflare Collect? When you visit our websites, Cloudflare may collect certain information to provide its services. This information may include:

IP Addresses: Cloudflare logs the IP addresses of visitors to provide security services and to improve its CDN services.

System Information: Information about the browser, operating system, and device used to access the websites.

Cookies: Cloudflare may use cookies to identify trusted web traffic and to prevent malicious activity on our websites.

Why Do We Use Cloudflare?

We utilize Cloudflare for the following purposes:

Enhanced Security: Cloudflare helps protect our websites against various security threats such as Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS) attacks, malicious bots, and other forms of cyber-attacks.

Improved Performance: Cloudflare's CDN speeds up the delivery of our website content to users by caching content closer to the user's geographical location.

Reliable Services: Cloudflare ensures our websites remain accessible and operate efficiently even during high traffic periods.

Sharing of Data with Cloudflare

By using our websites, some of your data will be transmitted to Cloudflare for security and performance purposes. Cloudflare acts as a data processor under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and is committed to protecting your data. For more information on how Cloudflare handles personal data, please refer to Cloudflare’s Privacy Policy available on their website.

Your Rights and Control

You have the right to know what personal data is being collected and to request the deletion or modification of such data. If you have any concerns about the use of your data through Cloudflare, please contact us at management@dasshorty.de to address your privacy rights.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or relevant regulations. Please review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
